CCB105 Digital Platforms — Week 6 · Asynchronous activity

How do I actually live on platforms?

Three short activities — no essays, no right answers, nothing to submit. Drag, slide, and tap your way to a clearer picture of your own platform behaviour. The whole thing takes about 10–15 minutes.

Activity 1 of 3

Your platform map

Drag each platform onto the map. The horizontal axis is about why you use it (personal vs institutional). The vertical axis is about how you show up (Resident — you leave a trace, vs Visitor — you come and go). Inspired by David White's V&R mapping framework.
